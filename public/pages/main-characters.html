BACKSTORY

“The mission doesn’t end until I stop breathing.”

Raised in Amarillo, Texas, Kyle Remington was brought up around firearms, farmland, and discipline. The son of a decorated Gulf War veteran, he learned to shoot before he could drive and enlisted at 18 straight out of high school.

Combat tours shaped him into a disciplined, front-line leader. When the outbreak hit U.S. soil, he volunteered for the first anti-infected units. Watching his comrades and civilians fall ignited a relentless resolve to eliminate the threat at any cost.

Now a First Lieutenant in a specialized task force, Kyle leads elite squads into the heart of infected zones. His mission: eradicate the undead menace and protect humanity’s last strongholds.

BACKSTORY

"Every scar reminds me why I keep fighting."

Lucía was born and raised in Veracruz, Mexico. After losing both her parents to gang violence, she fled to the U.S. at the age of 20 crossing the border alone with nothing but a small backpack.

Living undocumented, she endured years of harsh labor, poverty, and constant fear of deportation. When the outbreak spread, the government offered amnesty in exchange for military service. For Lucía, it wasn’t just a way out — it was a chance to fight back.

Forged by hardship and driven by purpose, she rose quickly through the ranks. Now one of her unit’s top exterminators, Lucía is known for her explosive combat style, tactical instincts, and the quiet intensity of someone who’s had everything taken.
